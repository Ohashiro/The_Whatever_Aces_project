Countries health system efficiency

This section shows the dependence between the Gross domestic product(GDP) and the HAQ (Health Access Quality) score, HDI (Human Development Index) score, and life expectancy in the selected countries. The HAQ is an index used to assess the global health level of a country. HDI is a score based on life expectancy, education, and per capita income indicators. Life expectancy is a statistical measure of the average life of a human-being.
